The Process

How It Works

Four simple steps from idea to inbox. No back-and-forth, no confusion.

1

Pick Your Product

Browse our top sellers, choose your garment color, sizes, and quantities using our easy order form.

2

Upload Your Artwork

Upload your logo or design file. Vector files preferred (AI, EPS, PDF). We can also help with artwork prep.

3

Approve Your Proof

We'll send a digital proof within 1–2 business days. Once you approve, we start production.

4

We Ship to You

Your order ships directly to your door in 7–10 business days after proof approval.
